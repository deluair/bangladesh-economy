Expanding the Export Base

While the Ready-Made Garments sector remains the crown jewel of exports, diversification into ICT, pharmaceuticals, and agro-products is key. Enhanced trade agreements and simplified customs procedures will open up new markets.

Policy Tip: Support promising non-garment firms with targeted incentives.
