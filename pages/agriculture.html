Modernizing Agriculture

Agriculture is evolving beyond traditional rice cultivation to include aquaculture, livestock, and high‐value crops. By adopting smart farming technologies and sustainable practices, Bangladesh can boost productivity and secure food for all.

  • Modern irrigation and precision agriculture.
  • Innovative agro-processing techniques.
  • Sustainable practices to combat climate challenges.
